About the Style Bar

Salon photo

Welcome to Audrey's Style Bar, Plant City's go-to spot for gorgeous hair in a space that feels like home. We're a cozy, boutique salon right in the heart of downtown on Collins Street.

Whether you need a fresh color, a crisp cut, or a blowout for date night, our talented stylists bring the perfect mix of skill and warmth to every single appointment.

Great hair should feel effortless, and so should the experience of getting it done.
